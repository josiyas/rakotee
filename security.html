Security Policy

Rakotee is committed to protecting your personal and payment information by following industry-standard security practices.

1. Secure Transactions

All payment transactions are encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and are processed through secure gateways such as PayPal.

2. Data Protection

  • We store customer information on secure servers with restricted access.
  • Personal data is only accessible by authorized staff and is used strictly for fulfilling your orders and providing customer service.

3. Account Security

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials. If you suspect unauthorized access, please contact us immediately.

4. Anti-Fraud Measures

We monitor all transactions for fraudulent activity. Suspicious transactions may be subject to delay, investigation, or cancellation.

5. Policy Updates

We may update our Security Policy as needed. Updates will be posted on this page and will take effect immediately.

6. Contact

For any security concerns, contact us at rakoteeholdings@gmail.com.
